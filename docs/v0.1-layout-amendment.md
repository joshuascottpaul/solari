# V0.1 Layout Amendment -- Zone-Based Collision Fix

**Status:** Approved  
**Date:** 2026-05-03  
**Amends:** V0 (Phases 1-15)  
**Reviewed by:** ripley (architectural review)

## Problem

Testing on a physical iPad at ~10 feet revealed two issues:

1. **Element collision.** Date (y=58%) drifts into time (y=47%). With time drift ampY=60 and date drift ampY=14, their vertical ranges overlap at mid-screen.
2. **Complications unreadable.** Slot text at 2.0vw (24px at 1180w) is too small to parse from across the room.

## Solution

Divide the viewport into four horizontal bands. Each element stays in its band. Increase complication font sizes.

```
Band 1  (0-24%)   moon        y=12%
Band 2  (24-60%)  time        y=44%
Band 3  (60-72%)  date        y=66%
Band 4  (72-96%)  slots       y=84%
```

| `#date`    | 2.0vw  | 4.0vw | 47px        |

Time (14vw) and moon (12vw) are unchanged.
